# Presentation Notes

## Opening

This project analyzes cosmetics data in Tableau to uncover trends in consumer preferences, brand performance, pricing, and skin-type suitability.

## What the Dashboard Shows

- Market Overview gives a high-level view of the dataset.
- Brand Performance compares sales and ratings across brands.
- Pricing Analysis shows how prices are distributed and grouped.
- Skin-Type Suitability highlights which products match different skin types.
- Consumer Preference Trends reveals which products users prefer most.

## Key Talking Points

- Strong ratings often align with strong sales.
- Skincare products in the sample data perform especially well.
- Price is important, but product fit and quality matter more than price alone.
- Skin-type segmentation creates clearer product targeting.

## Closing

The project provides a clear example of how Tableau can turn cosmetics data into business-ready insights.
