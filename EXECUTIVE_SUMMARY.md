# Executive Summary

Cosmetic Insights is a Tableau-based analytics project that examines cosmetics product data to identify market behavior, consumer preference, brand performance, pricing structure, and skin-type suitability.

## Summary of Findings

- Premium skincare products show strong performance in the sample dataset.
- Brand strength is closely tied to product quality and customer ratings.
- Mid-range products often perform well when they combine good pricing with strong reviews.
- Skin-type targeting improves product relevance and marketing precision.
- Product category remains a major driver of demand and sales.

## Business Value

The dashboards help businesses:
- identify high-performing products
- compare brands more effectively
- understand pricing opportunities
- target products by skin type
- support product and marketing strategy with data

## Recommendation

Use the dashboard suite as a decision-support tool for assortment planning, pricing strategy, and customer segmentation.
