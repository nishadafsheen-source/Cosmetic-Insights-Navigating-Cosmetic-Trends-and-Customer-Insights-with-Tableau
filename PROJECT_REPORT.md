# Cosmetic Insights Project Report

## 1. Introduction

Cosmetic Insights is a Tableau-based analytics project designed to study cosmetics product data and turn it into business-friendly insights. The project focuses on market trends, consumer preferences, brand performance, pricing behavior, and skin-type suitability.

## 2. Problem Statement

Cosmetics markets are highly competitive and diverse. Businesses need to understand which products sell well, what customers prefer, how pricing influences demand, and how to position products for different skin types. This project addresses that need through visual analytics.

## 3. Objectives

- Analyze cosmetic product trends across categories and brands
- Compare product performance using sales and ratings
- Identify pricing patterns across the product range
- Evaluate suitability for different skin types
- Support marketing and product strategy decisions

## 4. Data Overview

The dataset includes product-level attributes such as brand, category, price, rating, sales, reviews, skin type, and launch year. These fields make it possible to examine both business performance and customer preference.

## 5. Methodology

1. Collect and organize the cosmetics dataset
2. Clean missing or inconsistent values
3. Create calculated fields for metrics such as price bands and rating groups
4. Build interactive Tableau dashboards
5. Interpret patterns and summarize insights

## 6. Dashboard Insights

### Market Overview
Shows total products, average price, average rating, and category distribution.

### Brand Performance
Compares brands by sales, rating, and product count.

### Pricing Analysis
Shows how prices vary by category and how pricing relates to performance.

### Skin-Type Suitability
Highlights which products are most suitable for oily, dry, combination, sensitive, or normal skin.

### Consumer Preference Trends
Identifies the most preferred product types and categories based on ratings and sales.

## 7. Expected Outcome

The dashboards help stakeholders identify winning products, understand demand patterns, and make better product and marketing decisions.

## 8. Conclusion

Cosmetic Insights demonstrates how Tableau can be used to transform cosmetics data into actionable business intelligence. The project provides a structured view of market behavior and product performance.
