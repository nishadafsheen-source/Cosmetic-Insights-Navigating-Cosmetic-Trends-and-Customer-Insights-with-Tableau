# Insights Summary

Using the sample cosmetics dataset, the project highlights these example findings:

- Night Repair Cream has the highest sales and the strongest rating among the sample products.
- DermaLeaf performs strongly in skincare, especially for high-rated and high-sales products.
- AquaBloom shows consistent strength across skincare products, especially serums, toners, and masks.
- Makeup products show strong variety in pricing, with several mid-range products performing well.
- All Skin Types products such as sunscreen and perfume indicate broader market appeal.

## Sample Takeaways

- Premium skincare products tend to perform well when ratings are high.
- Brand reputation and product suitability both influence consumer preference.
- Price alone does not determine success; category fit and product quality matter.
- Skin-type targeting is a useful way to segment and market cosmetics products.
