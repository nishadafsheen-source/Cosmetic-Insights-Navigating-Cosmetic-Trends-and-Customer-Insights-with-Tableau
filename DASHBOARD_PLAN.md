# Dashboard Plan

## 1. Market Overview

**Purpose:** Provide a quick summary of the cosmetics market.

**Visuals:**
- KPI cards for total products, average price, average rating, and total sales
- Bar chart for category distribution
- Line chart for trends by launch year

**Filters:** Brand, category, skin type

## 2. Brand Performance

**Purpose:** Compare brand-level performance.

**Visuals:**
- Bar chart for sales by brand
- Scatter plot for rating vs. sales
- Table for top-performing products

**Filters:** Category, price range, rating range

## 3. Pricing Analysis

**Purpose:** Understand pricing structure across the catalog.

**Visuals:**
- Histogram of product prices
- Box plot by category
- Heatmap of brand and price band

**Filters:** Brand, category, launch year

## 4. Skin-Type Suitability

**Purpose:** Show which products fit different skin types.

**Visuals:**
- Stacked bar chart by skin type
- Treemap for category share
- Table of top suitable products

**Filters:** Skin type, brand, category

## 5. Consumer Preference Trends

**Purpose:** Reveal what customers prefer most.

**Visuals:**
- Rating distribution chart
- Top products by review count
- Category preference bar chart

**Filters:** Category, skin type, rating band

## Suggested Calculated Fields

- Price Band
- Rating Group
- Sales Band
- Popularity Score

## Final Output

Combine the sheets into a single Tableau story or dashboard workbook with navigation buttons and global filters.
